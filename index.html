Formerly known as CRITERIA, till 2010; ETAMAX is the inter-collegiate festival of Fr. C. R. Institute of Technology, Vashi. Ever since Criteria was initiated in 2002, over the past decade it has become one of the biggest college festivals of Navi Mumbai participated in by thousands of students each year. Attended in the past by celebrities like Shankar Mahadevan,Aditya Narayan and bands like Bhayanak Maut,Vajra,Indo Gypsies, Orcus, this year it promises to be much bigger!!!

Spanning over three days in the month of February, ETAMAX brings to you’ll an exciting mix of cultural and technical events from Robotics, Coding, Technical Paper Presentation to Group Dance, Band Event, Street Play, Film Making and much much more, giving a huge platform for students all over Mumbai n Navi Mumbai to showcase their talent and acumen.

Etamax’13 brings to you a new dimension that widens and extends the concept of utilizing your resources to the fullest to attain maximum efficiency!
